
## Proposition. Convex
$E|\varphi(X_n)|<\infty,\forall n$, then
  
  - MG $\Rightarrow$ $\varphi(X_n)$ is sub-MG
  - sub-MG. $\varphi\nearrow$ $\Rightarrow$ $\varphi(X_n)$ is sub-MG.

## Theorem. stopped at

## Doob's decomposition theorem
For S.P. $X_n$, $\exists$ MG $Y_n$,predictable $A_n$, that
$$
  X_n=Y_n+A_n
$$
unique to $Y_0$.
